2. **Demo Account**: Open a demo account and test your strategy
3. **Live Account Setup**: Complete KYC verification
4. **API Configuration**: Generate API keys with trading permissions
5. **Test Connection**: Verify API connectivity from your bot
6. **Paper Trading**: Test live execution with simulated funds
7. **Go Live**: Start with small position sizes and scale up

### Important Considerations
- **Diversification**: Consider using multiple brokers to spread risk
- **Compliance**: Understand tax implications in your jurisdiction
- **Security**: Never share API keys or passwords
- **Monitoring**: Set up alerts for margin calls and account activity

## üö® Risk Management with Brokers

### Forex Brokers
- **Account Segregation**: Ensure client funds are kept separate from broker's funds
- **Negative Balance Protection**: Prevents account from going negative
- **Margin Calls**: Set appropriate margin levels in your bot
- **Stop-Out Protection**: Configure your bot to close positions before margin call

### Crypto Exchanges
- **Risk Limits**: Set position size and leverage limits
- **Insurance Funds**: Choose exchanges with insurance for futures trading
- **Stop-Loss Orders**: Always use stop-loss orders
- **Withdrawal Limits**: Verify you can withdraw funds when needed

By selecting a regulated, reputable broker and following proper risk management practices, you'll be well-positioned for successful live trading with your Gold Currencies Bot.
